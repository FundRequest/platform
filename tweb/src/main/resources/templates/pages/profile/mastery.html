Categories of Expertise

The Progression System is designed around three Kaggle categories of data science expertise: Developer, Community, and X. Advancement through performance tiers is done independently within each category of expertise.

Performance Tiers

Within each category of expertise, there are five performance tiers that can be achieved in accordance with the quality and quantity of work you produce: Novice, Contributor, Expert, and Master.

a Developer Contributor, and a X Expert:
The highest tier you have achieved in any of the categories of expertise will be displayed on your profile and under your avatar across the site. Tiers are awarded on the basis of medals earned in each category.
